How did I get here?

My family moved to Hawaii from Washington when I was one month old, and we lived there until I was 5. As a Navy brat, it was lucky for me that my father had gotten stationed at Pearl Harbor.
I don't remember a whole lot, but vivid are the memories of clear water, warm rain, and magnificent sunsets!
